Frequently Asked Questions

Can you recommend an insurance agent or broker?

We are not a licensed insurance marketplace and do not make specific agent recommendations. To find a licensed independent broker in your area, visit the Independent Insurance Agents and Brokers of America directory at iiaba.net or search your state insurance commissioner's website for licensed agents in your ZIP code.

Can you tell me if my specific claim will be covered?

We cannot provide coverage determinations for specific claims or policies. If you have a dispute with your insurer about a claim, contact your state insurance commissioner's consumer assistance office. They provide free help to policyholders navigating claim disputes.

I found an error in one of your articles. How do I report it?

We take accuracy seriously and genuinely appreciate corrections. Please email us at editorial@insureshedii.com with the article title, the specific statement you believe is incorrect, and any source material that supports the correction. We review all submissions and update articles as warranted.

Do you accept sponsored content or paid placements?

We accept limited sponsorships and clearly disclose any commercial relationships in accordance with FTC guidelines. We do not allow sponsors to influence editorial content. For more information, email partnerships@insureshedii.com.

How quickly will you respond to my message?

We aim to respond to all messages within two business days. During high-volume periods, responses may take up to five business days. We read every message we receive, even if we cannot always respond individually to every inquiry.
